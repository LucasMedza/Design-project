Insects

Insects are living creatures that have six legs and three body parts, those being the head, the thorax and the abdomen. This is why spiders and scorpions are not actually insects.

Butterfly: Due to their bright colors and visits to flowers, butterflies are the most familiar of insects to humans. There are about 17,500 species of butterflies in the world.

Ants: a small insect, often with a sting, that usually lives in a complex social colony with one or more breeding queens. It is wingless except for fertile adults, which often form large mating swarms, and are known for industriousness.
