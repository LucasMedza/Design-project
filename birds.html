Birds

Birds are warm blooded animals that are special because they have feathers, they can fly, they have bills and their feathers regulate body temperature.

Raptors, in general, any bird of prey; the term raptor is sometimes restricted to birds of the order Falconiformes (hawks, eagles, falcons, and their allies).

Owls: A nocturnal bird of prey with large forward-facing eyes surrounded by facial disks, a hooked beak, and typically a loud call.
